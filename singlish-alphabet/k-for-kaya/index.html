Kaya Toast

Kaya toast is a staple breakfast item in Singapore, and it’s super simple to make:

Ingredients

Ingredients: butter, kaya, and a slice of bread. You’ll also need a butter knife, and a toaster.

Aburden! Liddat oso must say meh?

Limpeh

Butter first…

Toast the slice of bread, and while it is still hot, spread a thick layer of butter on it.

… then the kaya

Next a thin layer of kaya. Do this quickly, before the butter melts into the bread, so that the kaya and the butter mix - yum!

Enjoy!

Best served with Milo/ Kopi/ Teh!
